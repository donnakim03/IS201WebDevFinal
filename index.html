My Review

My Favorite Characters

  1. Ted Lasso
    • They did a good job in making Lasso a complicated yet likeable character.
    • His energy just radiates his great understanding about believing.
    • He just makes me happy.
  2. Roy Kent
    • His swearing...excessive yet so necessary.
    • His attitude... I'm just in love.
    • He is the one of the only rare men that I think pulls off a beard so well.
  3. Rebecca
    • I love that she has flaws and strengths and encapsulates this wide range of characteristics of being human and a woman.
    • She feels like a real person you can relate to, despite her status and wealth.
    • Queen... have you seen her arms???


My Favorite Episode

My favorite episode so far is Season 1 Episode 10, "The Hope That Kills You."

*Spoiler*

This episode showed the audience that results are not always expected. It made me reflect on what "believing" truly is and what I believe in for the long run. The sadness the team and all related personnels felt existed. Yet, the bond, the peacefullness, and other human emotions that make us feel okay could also be felt.
